Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2028-03-07

1. Information We Collect

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data. It operates locally on your browser and does not transmit any information to external servers.

2. Permissions Used

The extension may request the following permissions:

  • ContextMenus: To provide the option to search selected text on YouTube.

These permissions are solely used for the functionality of the extension and not for tracking or data collection.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension redirects users to YouTube's website for search results. Please refer to YouTube's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your data.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the extension listing.
